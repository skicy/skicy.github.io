Kumar Krishna Agrawal

I am a Resident at Google Brain as part of the Residency Program. My research interests lie in the intersection of Machine Learning and Robotics. I am passionate about developing algorithms for agents which can meaningfully interact with their environment.

I graduated from IIT-Kharagpur in 2017 with Bachelors and Masters in Mathematics and Computing. In the summer of 2016, I was a research intern at the MILA Lab under the supervision of Prof. Yoshua Bengio. Prior to that, I was a part of the Summer Research Expeditions, 2015 at Johns Hopkins University, where I worked under Prof. Raman Arora.
